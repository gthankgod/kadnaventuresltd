About Us

is an Incorporated Company with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC. 718619) with our Head Office at 8, Kasali Street Itire, Lagos and Branches at KSK Hotel and Event Center Alakia Ibadan and Benin Republic .
We are Nigeria owned company with over 15 (Fifteen) years of operating experience in Hotel Management, Agricultural Farming, Imports & exports, supplies of general goods, construction/pools, transporting, maintenance & general merchants. Our experience also covers business of Quarrying, Stone and Granite supplies, interior and exterior decorations, Providing loaders for manufacturing company etc.

KADNA VENTURES LIMITED has been operating successfully for more than 15 (Fifteen) years under our Late Chairman HRH. Oba Kolapo Ibiyemi (The Alara of Araromi Oke Odo, Ile Ife Osun State). He was an accountant and members of various reputable bodies within and outside the shore of Nigeria. He had over 39 years of experience with NNPC, NAKI CONSTRUCTION, DANTATA TEXTILES AND DANGOTE GROUP on the field as a Financial/Management accountant with his ability to lead and direct successfully. He rose from the position of an accountant to the highest level of his carrier and became the Special Assistant to the Vice President and Head of Special Duty of Dangote Group.
